ABOUT US

Sustainability, social equality and the environment are now business problems. And corporate leaders can't depend on governments to solve them..

As one of the largest non-governmental organizations in the country, SPARKS NGO focuses on high-quality, low-cost and replicable interventions to address gaps in the education system. Working directly with children and youth as well as through large-scale collaborations with government systems, Pratham programs reach millions of lives every year. SPARKS NGO Teaching at the Right Level approach has demonstrated proven impact on children's learning outcomes and is now being adapted to contexts outside India.
